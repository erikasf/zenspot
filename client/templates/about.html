About Zen Spot

ZenSpot is all about maximizing your precious time away from the office during the work day. Everyone needs to get away for a few mind clearing minutes, so lets make that time as productive as possible by packing in a delicious lunch and a calm place to collect your thoughts while you eat.

Every secret lunch spot is linked to a map and a spot overview page, which offers additional features like photos and lunch spot reviews. Start browsing for your private oasis and find the perfect zen spot near you!
