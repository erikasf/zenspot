Hiking Trail

This rooftop garden is all the way up on the 15th floor — you read that right, the 15th floor. It’s epic. If you walk along the edges of the space there are some incredible views, because, of course, you are 15 floors up. We can’t get over it. This view of the Transamerica Pyramid from the rooftop garden’s west side is pretty unbeatable.
